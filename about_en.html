History and Objective

Established with the objective of uplifting the farming community in koduvazhannoor, our Society has been a great support to the farmers for decades. We believe in cooperation and mutual growth.

Our mission is to provide financial security through financial products designed for modern farmers and to promote social welfare.

Vision

Our goal is to make every farmer in koduvazhannoor financially independent through high-quality banking services and transparent transactions. We strive to bring modern services to everyone while retaining the rural essence.

Mission

Our mission is to provide loans at low interest rates for the comprehensive progress of farmers and their families, and to raise their standard of living through social welfare programs.
